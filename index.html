Digital art installation

This is a digital art installation
The final project from my first year at UT with the theme of "Art and impact.

The installation is created with a goal of confronting users with a scenario that will trigger anxiety, discomfort, and self-consciousness. These are emotions that are commonly experienced by everyone but are still a difficult subject to talk about. By having multiple users experience this installation, we hope to create a sense of community, where people can share their personal experiences and understanding of similar situations to confront their thresholds of discomfort through collective reflection.
